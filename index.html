Sending a Still Photos from an iOS Live Photo on iPhones and iPads
Issue: iOS Live Photos contain sound and 3 seconds of video that you may not want to send to others.
Solution: You can send a still photo from any live-photo.
  1. Select the live photo(s) you want to send as you normally would.
  2. Tap the live photo icon watermark in the upper left side of the live photo.
  3. You should now see a line through the icon, indicating that the photo will send as a regular still photo and not as a live-photo.
